THE STORY

Music Rooted in Northern Uganda

17Chills is more than music — it's a movement. Born in the heart of Gulu, Acholi land, the sound fuses traditional rhythms with contemporary production to create something timeless yet fresh. Every track carries stories of love, resilience, community, and the vibrant spirit of the North.

Whether you're discovering Afro-soul for the first time or a longtime fan of Ugandan music, 17Chills invites you to feel the heartbeat of a new generation.
